About the Event

Welcome to Wonders of AI 2.0 Hackathon! A pivotal event hosted by KARE Open Source Society as part of Euphoria 2025: Techno-Management meets Sustainability, at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education.

Our Mission

The Wonders of AI Hackathon aims to bring together the brightest minds in technology to push the boundaries of artificial intelligence and create innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Team Specifications

  • A team must consist of a maximum of 5 members
  • Every team must have a unique team name
  • Each team should have a team representative
  • Inter-college and inter-specialization team members are allowed

Prizes

Prize Pool: INR 50,000

  • Participation certificates for all participants
  • Internship opportunity for best performers

General Rules

  • 24 hours to develop their solution
  • Projects will be viewed by the judging panel, and the top 3 projects will be selected
  • Finalists will be scored on originality, understanding of the problem statement, and the viability of the solution
